The bonus check is announced to sticky households one period in advance.
One period before the bonus arrives, households consume 6.1% of the bonus amount.
In the period that the bonus arrives, households consume 22.7% of the bonus amount.
One period after the bonus arrives, households consume 15.4% of the bonus amount.
Two periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 11.1% of the bonus amount.
Three periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 8.2% of the bonus amount.
Four periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 6.3% of the bonus amount.
