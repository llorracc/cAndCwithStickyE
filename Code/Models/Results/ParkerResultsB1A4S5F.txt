The bonus check is announced to frictionless households one period in advance.

In the period that the bonus arrives, households consume 16.5% of the bonus amount.
One period after the bonus arrives, households consume 11.7% of the bonus amount.
Two periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 8.7% of the bonus amount.
Three periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 6.5% of the bonus amount.
Four periods after the bonus arrives, households consume 5.0% of the bonus amount.
